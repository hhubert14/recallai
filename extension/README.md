RecallAI: AI-Powered Video Learning Assistant
RecallAI transforms educational YouTube videos into personalized learning resources. This Chrome extension automatically detects educational content, generates comprehensive summaries, and creates active recall questions to maximize knowledge retention.
How It Works
While you watch educational YouTube videos, RecallAI works silently in the background, using advanced AI to analyze the content. Once complete, you'll have access to:

Smart Summaries: Concise, well-structured notes capturing key concepts and important details
Active Recall Q&As: Custom-generated questions designed to strengthen your memory and understanding
Personal Knowledge Library: Organized collection of all your video learnings, easily searchable by topic

Why RecallAI?
In today's information-rich world, we consume more educational content than ever, but retain less. RecallAI bridges this gap by transforming passive video watching into active learning, helping you:

Save Time: No need to rewatch videos or take manual notes
Learn Better: Leverage proven active recall techniques for superior retention
Build Knowledge: Create a personal library of insights from every educational video you watch

Perfect for students, professionals, lifelong learners, and anyone who values knowledge retention in our increasingly digital learning environment.
